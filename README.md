# ☕ Coffee Sales Dashboard Project

**Project Challenge:**
A coffee distribution company aimed to gain deeper insights into their sales performance, customer behavior, and product preferences across regions. The goal was to turn raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard for data-driven decision-making.

**Objective:**
Enable stakeholders to understand sales trends, customer distribution, and product performance.

Highlight top-performing products and customer segments.

Identify opportunities for marketing, pricing, and customer loyalty improvements.

**Role:**
As a Business Data Analyst, I was responsible for:

Cleaning and analyzing sales, customer, and product data.

Designing a dashboard to visualize KPIs like total revenue, top countries, and customer performance.

Recommending data-driven strategies for growth and efficiency.

**About the Dataset:**
This dataset includes:

Orders data: Transaction-level sales with date, product, quantity, price, and customer details.

Customer details: Location, loyalty status, and demographics.

Product catalogue: Coffee type, roast level, unit pricing, and profitability.
